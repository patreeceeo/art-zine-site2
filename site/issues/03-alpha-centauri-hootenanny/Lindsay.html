About

I help organize a drink and draw. Recently, inspired by an event in Seattle called Dune Night, I set out to make a zine out of the artwork made each time we meet. This is that zine! I do think that physical zines a la Dune Night would be really cool, but… some of us only attend online and that is a lot to manage.
